Program Name
Headlong SMS Notifications

Program Description
Headlong uses SMS messaging to enable communication between users and an AI agent. Messages are sent and received in response to user-initiated interactions.

Message Frequency
Message frequency varies based on user interaction. You will only receive messages in direct response to your communications with the system.

Message and Data Rates
Message and data rates may apply. Please contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Support
For support, text **HELP** to our messaging number or open an issue at:
https://github.com/andyk/headlong/issues

Opt-Out
To stop receiving messages, text **STOP** to our messaging number at any time. You will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Privacy
We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. See our full privacy policy at:
https://github.com/andyk/headlong/blob/main/PRIVACY_POLICY.txt
